My Favorite Films

A collection of movies that inspire and entertain me

Transformers: Age of Extinction

Director: Michael Bay
Stars: Mark Wahlberg, Nicola Peltz, Jack Reynor
Year: 2014
Runtime: 165 min
Action Sci-Fi Adventure

After an epic battle that left Chicago in ruins, humanity picks up the pieces and moves on. But a group of powerful, ingenious businessmen and scientists attempt to learn from past Transformer incursions and push the boundaries of technology beyond what they can control. When a mechanic and his daughter make a discovery that brings down the Autobots and Decepticons once more, they find themselves in the middle of a war that threatens the entire planet.

Avengers: Endgame

Directors: Anthony Russo, Joe Russo
Stars: Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Mark Ruffalo
Year: 2019
Runtime: 181 min
Action Sci-Fi Adventure

After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War (2018), the universe is in ruins. With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe. The epic conclusion to 22 films brings the ultimate battle against Thanos, testing the Avengers like never before as they fight to save the universe and honor their fallen comrades.

The Amazing Spider-Man 2

Director: Marc Webb
Stars: Andrew Garfield, Emma Stone, Jamie Foxx
Year: 2014
Runtime: 142 min
Action Adventure Sci-Fi

Peter Parker runs the gauntlet as the mysterious company Oscorp sends up a slew of supervillains against him, impacting on his life. As Spider-Man, Peter faces his greatest challenge yet in protecting New York City from these threats while dealing with personal struggles in his relationship with Gwen Stacy and uncovering new clues about his parents' disappearance.
