My Favorite TV Shows

Binge-worthy series that captivate my imagination

FPJ's Batang Quiapo

Network: ABS-CBN
Stars: Coco Martin, Lovi Poe, Charo Santos
First Aired: 2023
Genre: Action, Drama, Crime
Action Drama Crime
Currently Airing

A modern adaptation of Fernando Poe Jr.'s classic film, this series follows Tanggol, a young man from the slums of Quiapo who gets involved in the criminal underworld. As he navigates the dangerous streets, he discovers his true identity and must choose between continuing his life of crime or fighting for justice in his community.

Money Heist (La Casa de Papel)

Creator: Álex Pina
Stars: Úrsula Corberó, Álvaro Morte, Itziar Ituño
First Aired: 2017
Seasons: 5
Crime Drama Thriller
Season 1 Season 2 Season 3 Season 4 Season 5

A criminal mastermind who goes by "The Professor" recruits eight people with certain abilities to carry out an ambitious plan: to print billions of euros in the Royal Mint of Spain. The group of thieves take hostages to aid in their negotiations with the authorities, who launch a major police operation to catch them. The series explores the intricate relationships between the robbers and the hostages.

Stranger Things

Creators: The Duffer Brothers
Stars: Millie Bobby Brown, Finn Wolfhard, Winona Ryder
First Aired: 2016
Seasons: 4
Sci-Fi Horror Drama
Season 1 Season 2 Season 3 Season 4

In 1980s Indiana, a group of young friends witness supernatural forces and secret government exploits. As they search for answers, the children unravel a series of extraordinary mysteries involving secret experiments, terrifying supernatural forces, and one very strange little girl with psychokinetic abilities. The series is a love letter to 80s pop culture while telling its own original story.
